Impact of covid-19 on student's physical health



BACK                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 NEXT
IMPACTS SOLUTIONS

  • Lost social interaction and lack of structured routines has lead to increased screen time, decreased physical activity, lack of concentration, anxiety and early depression.
  • School cancellations have heighten food insecurity for children, who depend on meal programs and increase vulnerability with the loss of school as a safe place.
  • Forced isolation and economic uncertainty have lead to increases in family violence, contributing to mental and physical trauma.

    •

  • Promote regular routines and schedules or help them create new ones in their new environment
  • If it is possible, create opportunities for children to play, to be physically active and to relax
  • Children should reduce their time of using smartphones, laptops and these types of gadgets.
